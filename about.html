About Me.
At 16, I was heading to Saint-Hyacinthe, in Québec, for an exchange year with AFS Interculture.
At 17, I was returning to Italy, knowing more about French language and Canadian culture.
At 18, I was finishing my scientific lyceum degree in Trieste (IT), right before heading back to Canada for my collegial studies.
At 19, I was studying Computer Science while interning in one of the most important textile research centers in North America.
At 20, I became president of my college's student union and started learning about artificial intelligence.

Now I'm 21, and I feel like I'm just getting started.
