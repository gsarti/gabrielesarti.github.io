My ​Projects.

Cégep of Saint-Hyacinthe's Student Union (RÉÉCSH)
The Cégep of Saint-Hyacinthe's Student Union (RÉÉCSH) is a nonprofit organization based in Saint-Hyacinthe, QC (CA). Its mission is to provide Cégep of Saint-Hyacinthe's students with social and political representation. Moreover, RÉÉCSH has the objective of protecting student's rights, provide students with useful services and promote their opinion inside and outside the cégep. RÉÉCSH is also a member of the Federation of Quebec College Students (FECQ), and contributes actively to the success of this organisation on the local and provincial scale.

I am currently the president of this organisation, the official spokeperson for the students of my college (roughly 5000 individuals). My main role is to ensure productivity and cooperativity inside the executive committee, organize tasks between executive members and promote project and campaigns for the association. For this reason, I am elected to preside both the executive committee and the administrative board of the student council.

﻿Federation of Quebec College Students (FECQ)
The Federation of Québec College Students (FECQ) is a nonprofit organisation, putting together more than 23 Québec colleges, for a total of more than 65 000 student members. Founded in 1990, this organisation constantly promotes student rights at provincial level, and entitles students members with a free juridical service (Juripop), alongside with a strong representation for their interests at a provincial scale.

I am currently the vice-president of one of the committees of this organization, the Committe of Institutional Affairs. Our role is to review most of the official documents of the Federation, in order to certify their validity or to modify them, following the guidelines provided by other members of the Federation. The task of this committee is crucial in order to keep the Federation up-to-date with the recent changes in the domain of student unions.

AFS Interculture Canada
AFS is a nonprofit organization, which prepares and activates future leaders, global citizens and changemakers with essential 21st century intercultural skills to engage, lead and collaborate effectively in different cultural settings. Their international exchange, study abroad and volunteer programs are supported by research-based intercultural learning journeys facilitated by trained AFS volunteers and staff.

I am currently volounteering in the same local committee that hosted me during my exchange year in 2013-2014. I am one of the social media managers for the committee and participate in the organization of many activities during the year, such as the arrival and the departure camp of exchange students.
