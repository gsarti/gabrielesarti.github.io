Italo Svevo Epistolary Corpus Analysis

In collaboration with Prof. Eric Medvet, head of the Machine Learning Lab @ University of Trieste and Cristina Fenu of Biblioteca Civica Attilio Hortis, I performed a rudimental topic modeling and sentiment analysis of Italo Svevo epistolary corpus. The results were published on the Svevian Museum's webpage and will be presented at the 2019 Lectures on Computational Linguistics of AILC @ University of Pavia, Italy.

Newscrapr

I created a simple scraper in order to extract large batches of articles from common newspaper websites. The project is currently functional for La Repubblica, a major italian newspaper, and is ready to be extended and improved by the community.
